About

Honoring the process of centering along with the elements of fire, water and air to create my own unique ceramic pot.
My work consists of a collection of handmade functional and decorative pottery that has been thrown on the wheel with either stoneware or porcelain clays.
All functional ware is food, dishwasher and oven safe.

Welcome to Clay, by Kimberleigh, a recreational tryst with clay!
